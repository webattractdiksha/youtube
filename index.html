Our Story

A seventeenth generation registani*, Neeraj has merged his passion for water and love for storytelling to create unique cultural tours in Jaipur. He is a graduate from Tufts University, Boston.

Heritage Water Walks is a passion project designed to showcase the rich cultural heritage of Rajasthan to curious visitors.

Being a living desert, it has the most distinctive culture and unique traditions, which remain undiscovered. Through Heritage Water Walks, it is our earnest attempt to present this ‘unique side’ of the desert state.

(*Registani = A person from Desert)
This native Rajasthani owned small heritage tour business is ranked #1 out of 343 Outdoor Activities in Jaipur on TripAdvisor. We are the 2021 winner of “Travelers’ Choice” award for the TOP 10% of ALL activities IN THE WORLD!
